# Product Idea: CalenMind AI

**Focus:** Turning messy unstructured data into a perfect schedule.

1. **Client (ICP):** Busy students and freelancers who manage multiple schedules (work, studies, personal life).
2. **Problem:** Manually entering classes, meetings, and deadlines from screenshots, emails, or messy notes is time-consuming and prone to errors.
3. **Solution:** An AI agent that integrates with Google Calendar. Users can send a photo of a printed syllabus, a voice note, or a text snippet, and the AI automatically creates structured events with reminders.
4. **Key Metrics:** * Number of events created via AI per user.
    * Monthly Active Users (MAU).
5. **Alternatives:** Manual entry, Apple/Google's basic "Siri/Assistant", simple OCR tools.
6. **Value Proposition:** "Turn any schedule into calendar events in seconds with just a photo."
7. **Segments:** University students, project managers, busy parents.
8. **Channels:** Chrome Extension, Telegram/WhatsApp bot, Product Hunt.
9. **Business Model:** Freemium (10 free syncs/month), Monthly subscription for unlimited power users.
