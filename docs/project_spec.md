# Project Specification: CalenMind (Full Idea)

## 1. Client (Ideal Customer Profile - ICP)
Busy students and freelancers who manage multiple overlapping schedules (university lectures, freelance deadlines, and personal appointments).

## 2. Problem
The high friction of manual data entry. Manually transferring classes and deadlines from screenshots, PDF syllabi, or messy handwritten notes is time-consuming, mentally taxing, and prone to human error.

## 3. Solution
A multimodal AI agent integrated with Google Calendar. Users can send a photo of a printed syllabus, a voice note, or a text snippet, and the AI automatically creates structured calendar events with pre-configured reminders.

## 4. Key Metrics
- **North Star:** Total number of calendar events created via AI per user.
- **Engagement:** Monthly Active Users (MAU).
- **Conversion:** Percentage of Free users upgrading to the Unlimited tier.

## 5. Alternatives
- Manual entry into Google/Apple Calendar.
- Basic Siri/Assistant (which fails with complex images or multi-event text).
- Simple OCR tools (which provide raw text but no calendar synchronization).

## 6. Value Proposition
"Turn any schedule into calendar events in seconds with just a photo."

## 7. Segments
- **University Students:** Managing semester-long lecture schedules.
- **Project Managers:** Turning meeting notes into calendar blocks.
- **Busy Parents:** Syncing school events and extracurricular activities.

## 8. Channels
- **Telegram Bot:** Primary interface for mobile users.
- **Chrome Extension:** For parsing emails and web-based schedules.
- **Product Hunt / Student Communities:** For initial organic growth.

## 9. Business Model
- **Freemium:** 10 free AI-syncs per month.
- **Subscription:** Monthly fee for "Power Users" (Unlimited syncs, priority AI processing, and advanced recurrence logic).
